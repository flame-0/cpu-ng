The Computer Professionals' Union (CPU) is proud to launch our new public website — a space for news, analysis, and organizing resources for Filipino ICT workers.

## Who We Are

CPU is a mass organization of ICT professionals, students, and advocates. We were founded on the belief that technology must serve the people, not just corporate interests or state surveillance.

Our members come from every corner of the tech industry: software developers, network engineers, cybersecurity specialists, IT educators, technical writers, and students in computer science and information technology programs.

## What We Stand For

We advocate for:

- **Digital rights and privacy** for all Filipinos
- **Workers' welfare** in the tech industry — fair wages, job security, and humane working conditions
- **Free and open source software** as a public good
- **Affordable and universal internet access**
- **Technology education** that empowers communities, not just corporations
- **Nationalist ICT policy** that prioritizes Philippine sovereignty and development

## How to Get Involved

Whether you are a seasoned professional or a student just starting out, there is a place for you in CPU. We hold regular meetings, workshops, and campaigns. Membership is open to all ICT workers and students who share our principles.

Follow us on social media or reach out at [secretariat@cp-union.com](mailto:secretariat@cp-union.com). Together, we can build an ICT sector that works for everyone.
